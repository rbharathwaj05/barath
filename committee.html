As per para 6 (a) of the All India Council for Technical Education Regulations 2009, Anti-Ragging Committee is constituted for RMK Engineering College.


It shall be the duty of the Committee to ensure compliance with the provisions of the Anti-Ragging Regulations as well as the provisions of any laws for the time being in force concerning Ragging. The committee shall also monitor and oversee the performance of the Anti-Ragging squad in prevention of ragging in the institution.


The committee shall oversee the actions to be enforced in the campus by display @ prominent places the AICTE placed on Anti-Ragging the acts which are deemed to be ‘Ragging and the punishments thereon’.


The Committee will meet frequently once in two months or as and when necessity arises and record the proceedings in the minutes books.


The circular may be exhibited in all notice boards and acknowledgement for service obtained from the Committee members.
